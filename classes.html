Information about Classes

Yoga

What will you be doing

Hatha yoga classed, based on the mat but using aids like a chair, blocks and straps where required. Each week, the class is different however we always start and end with a short relaxation session. All equipment is provided, but if you would prefer to use your own mat, that is fine. May want to bring a blanket for the relaxation in the colder months.

Who's it for

Mixed ability class, everyone welcome as long as you feel comfortable getting up and down off the floor. Everyone is encouraged to work within their own limits and not do anything their not comfortable with.

Venue

Gladstone Village Hall, Burton

Duration

90 minutes

Price

£7 per class or £36 for block of 6 classes

Chair Yoga

What will you be doing

75% of the class is chair based, taking movements to head, arms and legs. Each week, working on same movements to increase strength and mobility. Always end of the session with a short relaxation. All equipment is provided.

Who's it for

Those with reduced mobility or health issues that might have difficulties with the mat based class. Everyone is encouraged to work within their own limits and not do anything their not comfortable with.

Venue

Sports and Social Club, Burton

Duration

60 minutes

Price

£5 per class
