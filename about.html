About Andrew

Meet Andrew

Andrew is in the final stages of his Yoga Teacher Training with the British Wheel of Yoga. He's been practicing yoga for the past 6 years and decided in 2015 to change career and embarked on the journey of being a teacher.

Based on the Wirral, Andrew has regular classes in the village of Burton.
